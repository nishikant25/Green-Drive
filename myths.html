EV Myths Busted

❌ EVs are slow
✅ Actually very fast due to instant torque
❌ Charging takes too long
✅ Fast charging available
❌ EVs are expensive
✅ Lower lifetime cost
❌ EVs have low standerd and will leave your standerd
✅ Most modern EVs travel over 200–300 miles on a full charge, far exceeding the average daily commute.
❌ EV batteries die quickly and are costly to replace.
✅ EV batteries are designed to last for years (8-10 year warranties are common), with many outlasting the car itself.
❌ The electricity grid can't handle the load
✅ The grid can manage, as smart charging (e.g., charging at night) spreads demand.
❌ EVs are more prone to fire
✅ EVs are actually less likely to catch fire than petrol/diesel cars, with lower fire incidents per 100k sales, according to studies.
❌ EVs are worse for the environment due to power plants
✅ EVs have no tailpipe emissions and, even when accounting for electricity generation, produce significantly lower lifetime greenhouse gas emissions than gasoline cars.
❌ Electric vehicles are slower than gas cars
✅ EVs actually accelerate faster than many internal combustion engine (ICE) vehicles due to instant torque delivery
